Well Work Wonder

A quick puzzle game where players place pipes and filters to deliver clean water from a source to a village. Designed as a simple prototype to engage college students through play, strategy, and impact.

Brand-inspired colors are used for presentation only in this class prototype.

How to Play

Choose a piece from the tray, rotate it if needed, and place it on the grid to connect the water source to the village. Your final path must include at least one filter tile. If a leak appears, repair it to restore water flow.
